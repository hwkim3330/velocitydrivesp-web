Appendix B. YANG File Paths for Typical L2 Settings

Introduction

This appendix provides information on various Layer-2 VLAN settings.

YANG File Paths for Typical L2 Settings

Table B-1 provides a listing of typical Layer-2 YANG settings. More information, such as type ranges and min-max values, can be found in the YANG Library tab of the UI. The most popular VLAN settings are bolded in the table: VLAN-PORT-VID, VLAN-ID, VLAN-EGRESS-PORTS, and VLAN-UNTAGGED-PORTS.

Ingress flows are critically important considerations for a secure and predictable VLAN design, depending on if the switch's ingressing traffic is tagged or untagged, egressing as tagged or untagged, or VLAN filtered. Therefore, it is recommended to consult with an experienced Network Operator or Network Engineer to implement your network traffic flows.
